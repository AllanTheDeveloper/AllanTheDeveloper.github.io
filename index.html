Seattle

Seattle is located in the Pacific NW. On the east the city is bordered by Lake Washington and on the west by Puget Sound. With a growing population within the city limits and the Greater Seattle Metropolitin area, Seattle is a key location of major sports.

Seahawks

The Seahawks entered the NFL as an expansion team in 1976. In 1996 Paul Allen purchases the team and in 2010 Pete Carroll joined the team as Head Coach. The Seahawks have been to the Super Bowl three times, winning in Super Bowl XLVIII.

  1. City Population: approx. 650,000
  2. Metropolitan Population: approx. 3.5 Million
  3. SuperBowl Appreances: 2005, 2014, 2015
